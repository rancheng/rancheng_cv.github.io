DP-SLAM or NRDP-SLAM

We developed our efficient way to generate high performance global map form the submaps. By optimize DP-SLAM approach with nonparametric regression, we achieved the global map reconstruction from debris disoriented map. Please note, that our Nonparametric Regression DP-SLAM(NRDP-SLAM) method does not use IMU as ancillary fusion source.
